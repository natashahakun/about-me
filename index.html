About Me

I’m a Front End developer who loves to learn. Curious and open-minded, I am very excited about the constant opportunity for growth and progress in the tech industry.

Where are you from?

Orignially from a suburb of Philadelphia, I lived in Tampa, FL for six years before moving to Richmond. My favorite things about Richmond are its beautiful architecture, bike-friendliness, and welcoming people!

What’s your background?

Prior to learning web development, I was a teacher for three years and I've been a tutor and freelance editor for several years. My education background has helped me to grasp the importance of conceptual understanding and that has been a cornerstone in my process of learning web development.

What’s your dream job?

Being on a team with creative and engaging people working on projects that make a difference.

What are your favorite hobbies?

I love being active. Yoga, swimming, walking, biking! The downtown Y is an awesome place to hang out. I also love exploring new places in the city and eating delicious food (especially if it's prepared by someone else!).
